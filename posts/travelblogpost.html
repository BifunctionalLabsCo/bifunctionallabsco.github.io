JOURNEY TO UNTERSBERG

Back down in the heart of the Old Town, there are quite a few sights to see. A short distance from the market you will find the charming little cemetery, Petersfriedhof.

Right off the edge of Salzburg Altstadt, walk to the Stieglkeller to ride the FestungsBahn into the heart of the fortress. Take in panoramic views (in every direction), take a leisurely stroll inside, and visit exhibits that transport you through time and offer glimpses into the history and lifestyles of this town.

WITH THAT, OUR SHORT DAY IN SALZBURG IS AT AN END. OTHER MUST SEE SPOTS INCLUDE THE MIRABELL GARDENS AND DOM QUARTER IN OLD TOWN.
